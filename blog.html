Destination Guide: Waterfowl Hunting on the Mississippi Flyway

December 10, 2025 • Mark Waters

The Mississippi Flyway hosts millions of ducks and geese each winter. Key hotspots include the Upper Mississippi River National Wildlife and Refuge, the Atchafalaya Basin, and the Mississippi Delta. Visit in November for early spring‑wing migrations, and again in March for the late fall‑wing surge. Choose a raised blind near open water for optimal shot angles, and bring a high‑draw shotgun with 12‑gauge loads for safety and effectiveness.

Hunting Tips: Mastering Turkey Calls for Fall Success

November 5, 2025 • Sarah Feather

Turkey hunting demands precise sound work. Start with a box call for the classic cluck, then transition to a slate call for the classic “yelp” that triggers a gobbler’s territorial response. Pair a vocal decoy (recorded gobble) with a passive “roll call” to keep gobblers engaged. Practice timing – 2‑3 seconds between calls – and stay downwind of the flock to avoid spooking them.
