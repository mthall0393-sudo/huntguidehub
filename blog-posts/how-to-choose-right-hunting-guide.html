How to Choose the Right Hunting Guide

Choosing the right hunting guide can make or break your hunt. Here's what to look for:

Key Factors

  • Licensing: Ensure they're properly licensed for the state and species
  • Experience: Ask about years in operation and success rates
  • References: Talk to past clients
  • Gear: Check what equipment they provide
  • Safety record: Verify their safety protocols

Questions to Ask

  • How many hunters per group?
  • What's included in the price?
  • What's your success rate?
  • Do you provide lodging and meals?
