Waterfowl Hunting 101: Getting Started

New to duck and goose hunting? Here's what you need to know:

Essential Gear

  • Shotgun: 12 gauge, semi-automatic preferred
  • Waders: Keep you dry in the marsh
  • Decoys: Mallards and geese
  • Calls: Learn basic duck & goose calls
  • Blinds: Layout blind or pit blind

Where to Hunt

  • Public refuges: Many have daily draws
  • Private lease: More consistency
  • Guided hunts: Learn from pros

Tips for Success

  • Scout before you hunt
  • Know the regulations
  • Bring plenty of shells
  • Dress in layers
