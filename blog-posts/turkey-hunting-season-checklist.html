Turkey Hunting Season Checklist

Get ready for turkey season with this comprehensive checklist:

Before the Hunt

  • Purchase valid hunting license
  • Get turkey-specific tags
  • Scout your location
  • Check regulations for your state

Essential Gear

  • Shotgun (12 or 20 gauge)
  • Decoys (hen and jake)
  • Calls (box, slate, mouth)
  • Blinds or camo
  • Binoculars

Safety Check

  • Tell someone your plans
  • Carry first aid kit
  • Know your target
  • Be aware of other hunters
