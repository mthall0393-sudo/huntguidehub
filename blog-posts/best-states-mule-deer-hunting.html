Best States for Mule Deer Hunting

Mule deer hunting is a bucket-list experience for many hunters. Here are the top states:

Top Destinations

  • Colorado — High country hunts, OTC tags available
  • Wyoming — Huge public land opportunities
  • Montana — Great draw odds and trophy potential
  • Idaho — Quality over quantity
  • Utah — Big buck draws

When to Hunt

  • Early Archery: September
  • General Rifle: October-November
  • Late Muzzleloader: November-December
