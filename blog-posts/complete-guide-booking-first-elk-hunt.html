Complete Guide to Booking Your First Elk Hunt

Your first elk hunt doesn't have to be overwhelming. Here's how to plan it:

Step 1: Choose Your State

Colorado, Montana, and Oregon offer the best OTC elk hunting.

Step 2: Draw vs OTC

  • Draw tags: Higher success, harder to get
  • Over-the-counter: Easier to get, more hunting pressure

Step 3: Book Your Guide

  • Start 6-12 months early
  • Get references
  • Understand what's included

Step 4: Prepare Gear

  • Quality rifle or bow
  • Thermals for spot-and-stalk
  • Good boots and layers
