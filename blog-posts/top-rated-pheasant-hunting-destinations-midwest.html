Top Rated Pheasant Hunting Destinations

The Midwest is pheasant country. Here are the top destinations:

Best States

  • South Dakota — The king of pheasant hunting
  • Nebraska — Great public land
  • Kansas — Affordable and quality
  • North Dakota — Underrated gem
  • Iowa — Strong bird numbers

What to Expect

  • Great dog work (pointers & flushers)
  • Large tracts of public land
  • Affordable daily rates
  • Novice-friendly
