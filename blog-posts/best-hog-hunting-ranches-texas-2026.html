Best Hog Hunting Ranches in Texas for 2026

Texas remains the premier destination for hog hunting in the United States, offering vast private ranches, disparate habitats, and year‑round opportunities.

Top Ranch Picks for 2026

  • Lone Star Hunting Preserve – Stephenville, TX — Night drives with thermal optics
  • Rio Grande Hunting Ranch – Clint, TX — 2,500 acres with on-site processing
  • Longhorn Outfitters – San Angelo, TX — Family-friendly with cabin lodging
  • Blackwater Hunting Preserve – Tyler, TX — Sustainable hunting focus
  • Hill Country Trophy Ranch – Fredericksburg, TX — Boutique operation

Typical Pricing

Packages range from $1,200 for a day hunt to $4,500 for multi-day trips with meals and processing.

Best Time to Hunt

  • October–March: Cooler temps, night drives effective
  • Spring: Young hogs emerge
  • Summer: Early mornings/evenings only
